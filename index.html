Chocolate Muffin Recipe

Ingredients:

1 3/4 cup plain flour 2 tsp baking powder 1/2 tsp baking soda 3 tbsp cocoa 3/4 cup caster sugar 1 1/2 cups chocolate chips 1 cup milk 1/3 cup vegetable oil 1 egg 1 tsp vanilla extract

Pre-Steps: 1.Preheat oven to 180 Degrees C. Line a 12 cup muffin tin with patty pans. Steps: 1.In a bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, baking soda and cocoa. Add caster sugar and stir in 1 cup of chocolate chips. 2.In a jug, whisk together the egg, oil, vanilla extract and milk. 3.Pour the wet mixture into the dry mixture and fold together until just combined. 4.Fill the patty pans and sprinkle the extra 1/2 cup of chocolate chips over the top of the muffins. 5.Bake for 20-22 minutes.

Notes: Using a measuring cup to fill the patty pans is a sure way to have a uniform end product. I used a 1/4 cup measure for these muffins. You could use white chocolate chips for a variation. If you use a mini-muffin pan, this will make 24.
